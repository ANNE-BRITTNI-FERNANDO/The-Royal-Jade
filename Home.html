Jr. Suite

You'll find everything you need to enjoy your stay in Sri Lanka. In the Junior Suites at the Hotel TRJ Sri Lanka, you'll find one king-size bed (180x200) or two double beds (135x200 cm), satellite TV, air conditioning, a minibar, an electric kettle, and a balcony or terrace.


Price: 6000 LKR + taxes
Number of Persons: 2

Jr. Suite Superior

The Hotel TRJ Sri Lanka provides its Superior Junior Suites, 40-m2 rooms fully equipped to offer you the best amenities. These suites have two double beds (135x200 cm), a sofa bed in the lounge area, satellite TV, air conditioning, a minibar, an electric kettle, and a balcony or terrace with views of the sea.


Price: 10000 LKR + taxes
Number of Persons: 4

Family Suite

The perfect accommodations for enjoying a family vacation. These 77-m2 rooms have two bedrooms, one with a king-size bed (180x200 cm) and the other with two double beds (135x200 cm), a sofa bed in the lounge area, satellite TV, air conditioning, a minibar, an electric kettle, and a balcony or terrace.

Price: 17000 LKR + taxes
Number of Persons: 4

Suite

About Us

Welcome to The Royal Jade, your gateway to an unforgettable experience nestled near the enchanting Sinharaja Rainforest in Sri Lanka. Our hotel is more than just a place to stay; it's a destination where nature and luxury coexist harmoniously. At The Royal Jade, we pride ourselves on providing top-notch accommodations, outstanding service, and a wide range of amenities to make your stay truly memorable. Whether you're an eco-adventurer, a birdwatcher, or simply seeking tranquility, our hotel is the perfect starting point for your journey.

Learn More (Location: Near Sinharaja Rainforest)

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a harmonious blend of luxury and nature, where guests can escape the hustle and bustle of everyday life and reconnect with the environment. We are committed to sustainable practices, ensuring that our presence leaves a minimal ecological footprint on the pristine rainforest. Thank you for considering The Royal Jade as your retreat in the heart of nature. We look forward to welcoming you to our haven of serenity and natural beauty.

Our Team

Our team is comprised of passionate individuals who are committed to providing you with an unforgettable experience. We are dedicated to ensuring that your stay at The Royal Jade is nothing short of extraordinary.
